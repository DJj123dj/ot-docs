## Quick Overview
The /claim command is used to claim a ticket. You can also claim the ticket to someone else using the optional `[user]` property!
Claimed tickets can also be unclaimed & reclaimed!
**More info about ticket claiming <InlineLink isdoc="true" url="docs/system/ticket-flow#claiming">can be found here</InlineLink>!**
