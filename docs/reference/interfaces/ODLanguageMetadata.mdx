## Properties
<Divider></Divider>
#### `automated` <M color="red">boolean</M> <ApiBlock><ApiUrl url="js:boolean" label="boolean"/></ApiBlock> \{#prop-automated}
When `true`, the translator made use of some sort of automation while creating the translation. (e.g. ChatGPT, Google Translate, DeepL, ...)

> [**View Source**](https://otgithub.dj-dj.be/tree/main/src/core/api/modules/language.ts#L22)

<Divider></Divider>
#### `language` <M color="blue">string</M> <ApiBlock><ApiUrl url="js:string" label="string"/></ApiBlock> \{#prop-language}
The name of the language in english (with capital letter).

> [**View Source**](https://otgithub.dj-dj.be/tree/main/src/core/api/modules/language.ts#L16)

<Divider></Divider>
#### `lastedited` <M color="blue">string</M> <ApiBlock><ApiUrl url="js:string" label="string"/></ApiBlock> \{#prop-lastedited}

> [**View Source**](https://otgithub.dj-dj.be/tree/main/src/core/api/modules/language.ts#L20)

<Divider></Divider>
#### `otversion` <M color="blue">string</M> <ApiBlock><ApiUrl url="js:string" label="string"/></ApiBlock> \{#prop-otversion}
The version of Open Ticket this translation is made for.

> [**View Source**](https://otgithub.dj-dj.be/tree/main/src/core/api/modules/language.ts#L14)
